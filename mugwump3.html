MUGWUMP

CREARIVE COMPUTING MORRISTOWN, NEW JERSEY

THE OBJECT OF THIS GAME IS TO FIND FOUR MUGWUMPS HIDDEN ON A 10 BY 10 GRID. HOMEBASE IS POSITION 0, 0. ANY GUESS YOU MAKE MUST BE TWO NUMBERS WITH EACH NUMBER BETWEEN 0 AND 9, INCLUSIVE. FIRST NUMBER IS DISTANCE TO RIGHT OF HOMEBASE AND SECOND NUMBER IS DISTANCE ABOVE HOMEBASE.

YOU GET 10 TRIES. AFTER EACH TRY, I WILL TELL YOU HOW FAR YOU ARE FROM ECH MUGWUMP.
